The idea

You choose a password.
SeedRunner shuffles the entire BIP-39 list using its SHA-256 hash — assigning every one of the 2048 words a unique number.

Write those numbers on paper. Without your password, they are meaningless.
Re-enter the same password and the same mapping reappears — every time, on any machine.

How it works

Three steps to hide your seed.

01
Pick a password - DON'T ENTER YOUR SEEDPHRASE
This is your key — never write it down.
02
SHA-256 shuffles the list
Your password is hashed. That hash assigns every BIP-39 word a unique number from 1–2048.
03
Write down your numbers
Look up each word of your seed and note its number. All 2048 BIP-39 words are displayed at once with their numbers. No search, no input. Tuck the paper away safely.
04
Recover anytime
Re-enter your password → same mapping appears → look up your numbers → seed restored.

Security model

What it protects against.

Threat Protected? Why
Someone finds your paper ✓ Yes Numbers are meaningless without your password
Someone knows your password ✓ Yes Useless without the physical list of numbers
Someone has both paper and password ✗ No Seed is fully recoverable — protect both separately
Malware on your machine ✓ Yes Useless without the physical list of numbers
SeedRunner itself is malicious ✓ Auditable Fully open source — read every line before running

SeedRunner is an obfuscation layer — not a replacement for your primary seed backup. Always keep a secure copy of your actual words.

Features

Built for paranoid people.

🔒
RAM only
Nothing is ever written to disk. Close the window and it's gone.
🔄
Auto-rotating pages
The list cycles every 15 seconds to defeat screen recording.
#
Deterministic
Same password = same numbers. Every time. On any machine.
🔍
Sort by word or number
Find entries instantly whether you're encoding or recovering.
📦
Zero dependencies
Python standard library only. No pip, no packages, no surprises.
🖥️
Full list, always visible
All 2048 BIP-39 words are displayed at once with their numbers. No search, no input — just the complete shuffled list in front of you.
🖱️
Cursor disabled
The mouse cursor is hidden during display. No clicking, no copying, no selecting — the list is read-only by design.
⚡
Open source
Read the code. Audit it. Trust nothing you can't verify.
